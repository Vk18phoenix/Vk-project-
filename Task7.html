Lord Shiva is known as The Destroyer within the Trimurti, the Hindu trinity which also includes Brahma and Vishnu.[3][16] In the Shaivite tradition, Shiva is the Supreme Lord who creates, protects and transforms the universe.[10][11][12] In the goddess-oriented Shakta tradition, the Supreme Goddess Devi is regarded as the energy and creative power (Shakti) and the equal complementary partner of Shiva.[17][18] Shiva is one of the five equivalent deities in Panchayatana puja of the Smarta tradition of Hinduism.
